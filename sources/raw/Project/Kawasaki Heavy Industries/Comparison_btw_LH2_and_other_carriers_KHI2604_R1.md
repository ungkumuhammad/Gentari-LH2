Ref. Estimated ammonia demand for coal-fired boilers
in Japan
• Ammonia, which is less flammable, with higher co-firing in coal-fired boilers reduces steam generation and power
output.
• Coal-fired boilers nearing the end of their service life may see limited life extension due to Middle East conflicts.
However, when these boilers reach the end of their lifespan, significant demand for newly installed ammonia-fired
boilers is unlikely.
• Demand for ammonia for thermal power boilers will plateau at a certain level, and as more power plants reach the end
of their service life and are shut down, ammonia demand will also decline.
